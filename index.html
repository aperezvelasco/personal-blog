About Me

Bridging atmospheric science and high-performance computing

Work-life Summary

I am a Software and Data Engineer specialized in meteorological and climate data workflows. I design and implement robust pipelines for data acquisition, homogenization, transformation, and quality control using Python.

My expertise covers handling large-scale geospatial datasets (NetCDF, GRIB, Zarr), developing containerized services in operational environments (Docker, Kubernetes), and ensuring data interoperability. I work deeply on the modeling of forecasting and spatio-temporal downscaling tasks, evaluating the performance of both physics-based (NWP) and machine-learned outputs.
